    Early

    I was born and raised in Atlanta GA. I was a quiet kid but the teachers thought differently as they always said in my progress reports. I attended three elementary school in the SW Atlanta Area. It was during this time of my life also that I become more active in school activities. I ran track and was an active tutor for my teachers after school assisting in the lesson of the day to my peers who were not as clear on the topic. Going into Benjamin E.
